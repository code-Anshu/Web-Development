Overview of the Internship Program and duration

  • The internship program is unpaid for the candidates who do not opt for the interview process.

Interns are selected based on their application, resume, and in some cases, an interview. The selection process may vary for different internship positions.

The duration of internship is specified in thr internship offer letter provided to selected candidates.It varies depending on the nature of the internship.
